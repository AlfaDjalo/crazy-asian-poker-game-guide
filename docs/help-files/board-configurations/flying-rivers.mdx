**Flying Rivers**

Two flops are dealt, each with two cards.

Two turns are dealt, each with two cards.

One river is dealt with two cards.

To make a hand a player uses two cards from one flop and turn, one from
the river, and two from their hand.

Variants of Flying Rivers include:

- High/high/hand (3 points);

- High/low/hand (3 points);

- High/low/high/low/hand (5 points).
